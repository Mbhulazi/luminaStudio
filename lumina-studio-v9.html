Questions

Frequently
asked

Can I switch levels within one plan? +
Yes — you can switch between Beginner, Intermediate, and Master analysis depth at any time within your plan tier. The plan tier determines the number of analyses and export options; the skill level is always adjustable.
What types of portrait photos work best? +
Any portrait photograph works — studio shots, natural light, candid, and reference photos all produce strong results. Higher resolution images (at least 1000px on the shortest side) produce more precise zone mapping. RAW files are welcome on Atelier and Master plans.
How accurate is the style match? +
The style matching model was trained on lighting typology, edge quality, and tonal approach — not visual similarity. It identifies which historical painting tradition your reference most closely aligns with. It's a starting point for study, not a definitive classification.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Annual plans are refundable within 30 days of purchase. Your analysis history is retained for 90 days after cancellation.
Is there a student or educator discount? +
Yes — we offer 40% off Atelier for verified students and a group educator licence for atelier instructors covering up to 20 students. Contact us at edu@luminastudio.art.
How does the Grisaille & Notan Study limit work on the Free plan? +
Free accounts can generate up to 3 Grisaille & Notan studies. You can view and compare all 3 in full — the 6-stage slider, colour map, and palette all remain accessible — but PDF and TIFF export require upgrading to Atelier or Master. Once upgraded, study generation becomes unlimited and exports unlock immediately.
